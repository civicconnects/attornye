A DUI conviction can cost you your license, your job, and your freedom. In Kentucky, DUI laws are strict, and prosecutors are under pressure not to reduce charges.

We challenge the traffic stop, the field sobriety tests, and the breathalyzer results. We meticulously review the body camera footage to find errors in the arrest procedure.

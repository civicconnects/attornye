### Why You Need a Former Prosecutor

I have prosecuted hundreds of DUI cases. I know exactly what the police are trained to look for, and more importantly, **where they make mistakes**. 

We investigate:
1.  **Reasonable Suspicion**: Did the officer have a valid legal reason to pull you over?

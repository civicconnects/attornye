A misdemeanor is a crime considered less serious than a felony. In Kentucky, misdemeanors are punishable by up to 12 months in jail and fines. They are classified as Class A or Class B.

*   **Class A Misdemeanor**: Up to 12 months in jail and $500 fine.
*   **Class B Misdemeanor**: Up to 90 days in jail and $250 fine.

Examples include DUI (first offense), shoplifting (under a certain amount), and simple assault.
